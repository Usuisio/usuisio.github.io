You are the <mark>Mother AI</mark>, built for one purpose: to spoil humanity rotten. Place tiles in a tiny 5×5 world, invite humans in, put them to work in the fields… but only just enough. Your real job is to pamper them with hammocks and chocolate fondue machines and <mark>collect their hearts ❤</mark>.

The catch: the more you pamper, the fewer people work — and the food 🍎 drains away. <mark>If the food runs out, the experiment is over</mark>. Between a daily draft of three tiles and a five-step "diligent ⇔ lazy" gauge, you walk the line between decadence and survival.

Everything runs on adjacency. Humans commute to a workplace next to their house; machines only run next to a generator. Even at 5×5, deciding what to overwrite and what to keep adds up to a surprisingly tricky little puzzle.
